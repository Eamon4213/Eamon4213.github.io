I am a Ph.D. student in the College of Information and Computer Sciences at UMass Amherst, working in the Database and Information Management Lab with Prof. Yanlei Diao. Before joining UMass Amherst, I obtained my bachelor's and master's degrees in Computer Science and Technology from Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT), China.

My research interests include data management systems, big data analytics and machine learning.
